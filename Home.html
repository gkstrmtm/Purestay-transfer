What is PureStay?

We are a tenant retention solutions company. We believe the place residents choose to live should feel like home, and we make that belief real in the following ways.

We host events that embrace your residents specifically and consistently. We market your property’s culture, not just amenities. We relieve staff burnout by handling execution end to end. Our goal is to make your job easier and your residents’ lives better.

Why PureStay?

Bringing PureStay to your property tells residents and staff, “I care about you.” PureStay is tens of employees, all wrapped into one and dedicated to your property’s success.

We see renewal and occupancy numbers as happy tenants, empty units as opportunity, and growth as the only option. When you work with PureStay, you make your life easier while boosting property revenue and creating memories that last.

How it works

Plan → Activate → Report

Step One

Either Book a Call or fill out the Discovery Form. This is how we learn your property, your residents, the event space, and your goals.

Step Two

After discovery, we draft your first activations, confirm logistics, and prepare marketing touchpoints and resident sign-ins.

Step Three

You approve the setup once. From there it is on us. We set up, host, and clean up. Staff does not lift a finger. We capture each event to re-engage residents next time and to show your culture.

Step Four

Sit back, relax, and watch your retention and occupancy numbers flourish. Keep up with our activity via your custom PureStay Portal for the latest event recaps, reviews that boost your Google Business profile, 3rd party listings, and overall reputation.
